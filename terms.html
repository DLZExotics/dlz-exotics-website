Sales Terms

Buyer information & sales policy

Please read before reserving or purchasing an animal from DLZ_EXOTICS.

Availability

All animals listed as available are offered first-come, first-served. Availability may change until a deposit or full payment is received.

Deposits & Holds

A non-refundable deposit may be required to hold an animal. Holds are not guaranteed without a confirmed deposit.

Payment

Full payment must be completed before pickup or shipping. Payment options and final totals are confirmed through direct message.

Shipping

DLZ_EXOTICS is based in Connecticut. Local pickup is available. Shipping is available weather permitting. Buyer must provide a zip code for a quote.

Live Arrival

Live arrival is guaranteed when shipping is arranged and approved by DLZ_EXOTICS. Buyer must be available for first delivery attempt.

Genetics

Recessive genetics are based on pairing records. Multi-gene visual IDs are listed as accurately as possible.

By purchasing from DLZ_EXOTICS, the buyer confirms they understand proper ball python care, heating, enclosure setup, feeding, and safe handling. DLZ_EXOTICS reserves the right to refuse a sale if the placement does not appear to be in the animal's best interest.
